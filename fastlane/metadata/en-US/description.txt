Soccer Assistant Coach helps coaches and team managers run their teams more efficiently — from practice planning to live game day.

BUILD YOUR LINEUP
Create starting lineups and formations in seconds. Choose from popular formations like 4-3-3, 4-4-2, 3-5-2, and more. Drag and assign players to any position on the pitch.

MANAGE YOUR ROSTER
Create and manage multiple teams with seasonal rosters. Add players, track positions, and keep your squad organized all season long.

TRACK GAME METRICS
Record simple in-game metrics and attendance during matches. Review performance data after the game to inform your next selection.

ORGANIZE EVERYTHING
Archive past teams and seasons, then restore them when needed. Export and import team data via CSV for easy backups or sharing between devices.

LOCAL-FIRST DESIGN
Your data stays on your device. No account required, no subscription, no data sent to the cloud — just you and your team.
